# UCU CMS Guide: Managing "About Us" Pages

This document provides a detailed guide on how to use the Content Management System (CMS) to manage the **About Us** section of the Universal Corporate University (UCU) website.

You can copy the contents of this file into a Google Doc for easier sharing and collaboration.

---

## 1. Accessing the About Us Dashboard

1.  Log in to the UCU CMS Dashboard.
2.  Navigate to the **About Us** section (or click on **Specialized Pages** if labeled as such).
3.  You will see the **About Us / Specialized Pages** dashboard. This is your central hub for managing all "About" related content.

## 2. Dashboard Overview

The dashboard lists all existing pages categorized by their type:
*   **Generic**: Standard content pages (e.g., Vision, Mission, History).
*   **Leadership**: Special pages layout for Board of Directors or Team lists.
*   **Industry**: Specific layout for Industry Approach content.
*   **Founder**: Dedicated layout for the Founder's Message.

### Actions Available
*   **Create New Page**: Click the "Create New Page" button at the top right.
    *   Select the **Page Type** (Generic, Leadership, Industry, or Founder).
    *   Enter a **Page Title**.
    *   Click **Create Page**.
*   **Edit**: Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon) next to any page to modify its content.
*   **Delete**: Click the **Delete** button (trash icon) to remove a page. *Warning: This action is permanent.*

## 3. Editing Pages

Different page types have different editors tailored to their specific layout needs.

### A. Generic Page (Standard CMS)
Use this for most content-heavy pages like "Vision & Mission" or "Our Story".

**1. Main Header**
*   **Page Title (Display)**: The title shown at the top of the page.
*   **Banner Image**: Upload a high-quality banner image for the page header.
*   **Description / Intro Text**: A brief introductory paragraph displayed below the title.

**2. Content Sections**
You can build the page by adding multiple sections.
*   Click **Add Section** to create a new block.
*   **Section Control**: Use the trash icon (top-right of the card) to delete a section.

**Section Types**:
Change the "Type" dropdown to switch layouts:
*   **Content Block**: Standard text and image.
*   **Vision / Mission**: Special formatting for vision/mission statements.
*   **Timeline Event**: For history pages (requires "Gallery" for images).
*   **Stat / Counter**: Displays large numbers (e.g., "500+").
*   **Brand Logos Grid**: For displaying partner logos.
*   **Founder Profile / Team Member**: Specific cards for people.

**Section Fields**:
*   **Heading**: The section title. *Tip: Some inputs allow highlighting text colors.*
*   **Subheading**: Used for Job Titles or extra details in Stat/Person cards.
*   **Content**: A Rich Text Editor allows you to format text (Bold, Italic, Lists) similar to Word.
*   **Image**: Upload a primary image for the section.
*   **Gallery Images**: (For Timeline/Logos) Upload multiple images in a grid.

---

### B. Founder Message Page
This is a specialized page designed strictly for the Founder's Message layout.

**1. Header Section**
*   **Page Heading**: Title of the page (e.g., "Message from the Founder").
*   **Founder Details**: Enter Name, Title, and Organization.
*   **Founder Image**: Upload a professional portrait.

**2. Message Content**
*   **Message Body**: Use the Rich Text Editor to write the letter/message.
    *   *Note: You can paste content from Word/Docs, but ensure formatting is clean.*

**3. Signature Section**
*   Define the Name, Title, and Organization that appears at the bottom of the letter as a sign-off.

---

### C. Leadership & Industry Pages
These pages follow a similar pattern but are optimized for their specific content (e.g., lists of team members or industry pillars). content management works similarly to Generic pages where you add/edit specific items.

## 4. Saving Your Work

*   **Save Changes**: Always click the **Save Changes** button at the top right after making edits.
*   **Unsaved Changes Warning**: If you try to leave the page without saving, the system will warn you. You can choose to "Leave without Saving" or "Save & Leave".

---

## 5. Tips for Best Results
*   **Images**: Always use high-resolution images but try to keep file sizes optimized for web speed (e.g., under 500KB if possible).
*   **Text Formatting**: Use the formatting tools (Bold, Bullet points) to break up large blocks of text for better readability.
*   **Page Titles**: Keep titles concise and descriptive.
